## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: I see a flat line in the chart. Is something broken?
**A:** No! A flat line means the numbers are staying the same. For example, if appointments are steady at 20 per day, you'll see a flat horizontal line.

### Q2: What if I see numbers as 0 or empty charts?
**A:** This means there's no data for that category yet. For example:
- No revenue = No pharmacy sales yet
- No appointments = No appointments booked yet

### Q3: Can I print these charts?
**A:** Yes! Use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P) to print the page.

### Q4: Which chart is most important?
**A:** All charts tell you something different, but if you're short on time:
1. Check **Low Stock Medicines** first (urgent!)
2. Look at **Revenue Trends** (business health)
3. Review **Appointment Trends** (patient flow)
